About me

Hamid Rajabi is a second year PhD student who is working on Wireless Sensor Networks, Cyber-Physical system, and Internet of Things (IoT) at University of California, Merced. He works under supervision of Dr. Alberto E. Cerpa at the department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) where his main current focus is on Occupancy Estimation for Smart Buildings using Neural Networks and Learning Compression. Meanwhile, he has been holding TA position for four consecutive semesters in the same department for the course Computer Networks. Before coming to Merced, Hamid finished up his Master's degree in the ECE department of University at Albany (SUNY) with focus on Remote radio Sensing.
